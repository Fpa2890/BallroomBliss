Ballroom Dance

Ballroom dance is a refined and expressive form of partnered dance that blends technique, musicality, and artistry. Originating from European social dances, it has evolved into both a competitive sport and a popular social activity. Ballroom dance is divided into two main styles: International Style, which includes Standard and Latin, and American Style, which features Smooth and Rhythm. Each dance carries its own unique character, from the elegance of the Waltz to the fiery passion of the Tango.
