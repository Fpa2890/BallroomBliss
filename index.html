Ballroom Dance

Ballroom Dance is a timeless and elegant form of partnered dancing that combines grace, rhythm, and technique. It encompasses a variety of styles, each with its own unique characeteristics, footwork, and cultural influences.

American Style

American Style Ballroom is a dynamic and expressive form of ballroom dance developed in the United States. It is divided into two main categories: Smooth and Rhythm.

International Style

International Style Ballroom is a highly structured and globally recognized form of ballroom dance, divided into two main categories: Standard and Latin.
