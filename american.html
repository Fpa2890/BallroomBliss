American Style

American Style ballroom dance is a versatile and expressive form of dance that includes two main categories: Smooth and Rhythm. American Smooth emphasizes fluid movement, open positions, and dynamic storytelling, allowing dancers greater freedom of expression compared to its International counterpart. American Rhythm focuses on lively, rhythmic movements with a strong emphasis on hip action and musical interpretation.
